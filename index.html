Device-to-Device messaging made simple.

  • Mobile to Mobile
    Now you can easily write applications that work across two or more phones, without also writing and maintaining a central server. We'll help you with device discovery and cross-device messaging.
  • Cross Platform
    Use Junction to connect most modern platforms, including web, Android, and iOS for iPhone and iPad.
  • Rapid Development
    Junction is easy to learn, easy to debug, and easy to deploy. Develop your application using simple message-passing techniques or using our robust state management framework.
  • Open Source
    Junction is free and open source. We also leverage existing open standards for improved compatibility and ease of use.
